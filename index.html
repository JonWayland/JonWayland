About Me

My name is Jon, and I love data. I'm the Director of Data Science at Florida Blue and I specialize in bridging data science solutions with business initiatives. I spend most of my work days collaborating with technical teams (i.e., my team) and various business functions. Over the course of my career, I've spent a lot of time thinking about how to best bring data clarity to decision-makers.

Click here to view my Resume!

I have two masters degrees - one in applied math and one in business. I obtained my first masters from Indiana University of PA in 2014 (applied math) and my second masters from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (business). I also have completed two multi-month certificates - one in machine learning engineering from Udacity in 2017 and one in data science from HarvardX on edX in 2019.

The best part about me is my family. I have a beautiful wife and two children, a daughter and a son. We live in the sunny state of Florida and enjoy occasional trips to Disney World and more frequent trips to the beach.

Statistical Storytelling

A core aspect to my job as a data scientist is to explain data concepts in a way that resonates. More often than not, the audience for my descriptions come from a non-technical, non-mathematical background. As a result, I find myself using analogies and exercising storytelling to get points across. As most of this is done informally, I have also put together various stories over the years, some fictional and others realistic, to describe certain data concepts.

Note: Some of these stories were published on other platforms, including Quora and LinkedIn, but were all originally authored by Jon Wayland.
